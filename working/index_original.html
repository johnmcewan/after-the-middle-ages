Medieval Seals after the Middle Ages

‘Medieval Seals after the Middle Ages’ is a conference on the history and legacy of books of seals, organized by the University of Kansas, with support from Oxford University and the Bibliographical Society of America. The event will happen in person at the University of Oxford and online
