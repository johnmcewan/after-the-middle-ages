The Enduring Legacy of the Sigillum

This hybrid conference brings together leading scholars at the University of Oxford and online to examine the afterlife of medieval seals. Beyond their primary administrative roles, these artifacts have persisted through the centuries as objects of antiquarian fascination, artistic inspiration, and historical evidence, forming a living archive of medieval identity.
